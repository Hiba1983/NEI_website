Below is information about and links to important information about your benefits coverage related to the coronavirus.

COVID-19 Over-the-Counter (OTC) Home Test Kits

Effective January 15, the Plan will cover up to eight OTC COVID-19 tests for each covered member of your family if the test is not ordered by a health care provider. This latest announcement explains the details and eligibility requirements.

Coverage at a Pharmacy

Once COVID-19 vaccines are available from local retail pharmacies, the Plan will cover the vaccine under its Prescription Drug benefit. The Plan will cover 100% of the administrative costs for all COVID-19 vaccines administered at local retail pharmacies.

Coverage at a Doctor’s Office, Medical Facility, or Clinic

The Plan covers vaccines administered at a doctor’s office, medical facility, or clinic at 100% of administrative costs when received from in-network providers and 100% of reasonable administrative costs when received from out-of-network providers. The Plan’s payment of these administrative costs is covered under the Plan’s Wellness Benefit and is not subject to the Plan’s annual deductible.
