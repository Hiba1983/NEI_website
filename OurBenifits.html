NEI Benefits

National Elevator Industry Benefit Plans

The National Elevator Industry Pension, Health and Annuity and 401(k) Retirement Plan Once you are eligible for coverage, benefits are provided to you, your spouse, and eligible children. If you’re new to coverage, you must enroll yourself and your family.
The Plan also provides financial protection for you and your family through short-term disability benefits and life and accident insurance coverage.

Applying for Benefits:

You must submit copies of the following documents, as applicable:
1) If you are married: Proof of your age and your spouse’s age, such as birth certificates A copy of your certified marriage license
2) If you are divorced: A copy of your divorce decree and any settlement/property agreements. A copy of your qualified domestic relations order (QDRO)
3) If you are widowed: A copy of your spouse’s certified death certificate.
4) If you are salaried: A copy of proof of employment dates and termination.
5) If you are disabled: A copy of your Social Security disability award notification.
6) If you served in the military during your elevator career: A copy of your Form DD214.
7) If you are on Medicare: A copy of your and/or your spouse’s Medicare card.
